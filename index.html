Services

Services

Equipment Maintenance

Regular scheduled maintenance is essential to keep your IT equipment running smoothly and prevent potential issues. We can create a customized maintenance schedule based on your specific needs, which may include tasks such as software updates, hardware cleaning, and system checks

Outsourced IT Support

Our help desk support services provide your employees with a single point of contact for IT support. Our technicians are available via phone, email, or chat to quickly resolve issues and answer questions.

With our remote support services, our technicians can access your systems remotely to diagnose and resolve issues without the need for an on-site visit. This allows us to resolve issues quickly and efficiently, minimizing downtime and disruption to your business.

In the event that a technician needs to be on-site, we can dispatch a qualified technician to your location to resolve the issue. Our technicians are trained to diagnose and repair a wide range of IT issues, including hardware and software issues.

Hardware Upgrades

We can provide a wide range of hardware upgrades, including new processors, additional memory, faster storage, and improved graphics cards. These upgrades can help improve the performance of your systems and extend their useful life.

If your hardware is outdated or beyond repair, we can recommend and install replacement hardware. We can also help you recycle your old hardware in an environmentally responsible way.

Computer Setup

Configuring a new PC or workstation can often be time consuming and difficult. We can help set up new workstations for your employees and make sure they have all the tools they need on their computer.

Flat, Monthly Rate

We can develop a fixed monthly support plan for your business that allows you to budget for technology effectively.

Network Setup

Before designing a network installation, Rapid IT will assess your existing network infrastructure to identify areas for improvement and potential issues. This assessment will help us determine the best approach to designing your new network. We will work with you to design a network that is tailored to your unique business environment. We will consider factors such as the size of your office, the number of users, the types of applications you use, and your budget.
